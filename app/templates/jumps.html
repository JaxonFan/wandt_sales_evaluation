An account whose last 4 weeks jumped to 2×+ its normal level — the higher of its recent pace and its seasonally-adjusted year-ago — has its windfall withheld by default (a 10× is usually the customer growing their own business, not the rep's win). Confirm Rep won it to release the windfall into growth, or leave it Customer-driven to keep it withheld. The Jump × is the whole-account multiple that triggered the flag; Account 4 wks / Normal level are account-level, while Rep share (sold vs that rep's own slice of the bar) and Windfall held are each rep's proportional slice — which is why the × is the same for everyone who touched it. Smaller, ordinary overage isn't listed here — it pays automatically. A likely timing badge means the account's last 3 months are flat vs the prior 3 — so the spike is probably a recurring order that landed on a different week, not real growth → usually leave it customer-driven / withheld.
